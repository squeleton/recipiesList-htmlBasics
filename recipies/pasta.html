Pasta

Description

This recipe is too easy, will just put water whith salt in the cooking pot
then after is boiled put pasta there and let it rest for aprox 12 mins
then you slip the water, thats it you put the tomato at your choice.

Ingredients

  • Pasta a eleccion
  • Queso rayado
  • Pasta de Tomate
  • Sal
  • Aceite

Steps

  1. Step 1: Boil Water. Start with a very large pot of water,
    about 6 quarts per pound of pasta.
  2. Step 2: Add Salt.
  3. Step 3: Add the Pasta.
  4. Step 4: Stir.
  5. Step 5: Taste the Pasta.
  6. Step 6: Drain.
  7. Step 7: Removing Ravioli.
  8. Step 8: Stir in the Sauce.
